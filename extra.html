Your Radiant Skincare Journey

Tip: Lukewarm water keeps skin soft!

1. Wash Your Face

Cleanse away the day’s dirt with a gentle cleanser. Use soft, circular motions for 60 seconds, rinse with lukewarm water, and pat dry gently.

Tip: Double-cleansing prevents breakouts!

2. Remove Makeup

Use micellar water or cleansing oil to remove all makeup before cleansing. Hold a cotton pad over eyes to dissolve stubborn makeup gently.

Tip: Hydration boosts your glow!

3. Don’t Skip Moisturizer

Hydrate with a moisturizer suited to your skin type. Apply with upward motions to lock in moisture and support overnight repair.

Tip: Start treatments slowly!

4. Add Treatments

Use serums or spot treatments like salicylic acid or niacinamide. Apply sparingly and follow with moisturizer to avoid irritation.

Tip: Gentle touch soothes skin!

5. Move Softly

Apply products with gentle, circular strokes. Avoid harsh rubbing to keep your skin calm and protected from irritation.

Tip: Masks add a spa-like vibe!

6. Personalize

Add a face mask, facial oil, or calming mist to make your routine special. Adjust products seasonally for your skin’s needs.
