Do’s & Don’ts of Sunscreen

  • Do apply at least 20 mins before going out.
  • Don’t skip areas like ears, neck, and lips.
  • Do reapply every 2–3 hours.
  • Don’t rely only on makeup with SPF.
  • Do choose the correct SPF for your skin type.
  • Don’t forget water-resistant sunscreen if swimming.
